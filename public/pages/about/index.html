About

Mar 30, 2017  

I am Research Associate at Queensland Univerity of Technology and a Visiting Scientist at the CSIRO Data61. I work on applied statistics problems with industry partners as well as meta-projects that aim to make data analysis easier. My work interests are R, data wrangling, the discipline of reprodicble analysis, forecasting from simulations, and visualisation in AR/VR.

In my spare time I help organise the Brisbane Users of R Group (BURGr), and have put on 2 R unconferences is Brisbane.

The best way to get my attention is to tag @milesmcbain on Twitter.
